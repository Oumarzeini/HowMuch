FAQ's

How do i post a product

After signing up, navigate to your dashboard and click 'Post New Product'. Add details including title, description, price, and photos. Once submitted, your product will appear in search results and on your profile.

Can i message Traders directly ?

Absolutely! Our direct messaging system allows you to communicate privately with any trader on the platform. Simply visit their profile and click the 'Message' button to start a conversation.

Is my data safe ?

We take security seriously. HowMuch uses bank-level encryption to protect your personal information and transaction details. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

What payment methods are supported ?

While we facilitate connections between traders, payments are arranged directly between parties. We recommend using secure payment methods like bank transfers, PayPal, or escrow services for high-value transactions.
